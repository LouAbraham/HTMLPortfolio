The Best Movies As Adjudicated by Lou

My Top 3 Movies of all-time

  • Star Wars: Episode V - The Empire Strikes Back (1980)

    Directed by Irvin Kershner, this film is a masterpiece of storytelling and character development, featuring iconic performances and groundbreaking special effects.

  • The Godfather (1972)

    Directed by Francis Ford Coppola, this film is a classic crime drama that explores the complexities of family loyalty and power within the Mafia.

  • Casablanca (1942)

    Directed by Michael Curtiz, this romantic drama set during World War II is renowned for its memorable quotes, strong performances, and timeless love story.
